Story About King of the Pirates "Luffy"

Bio of King of Pirates

Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, and dreamt of being a pirate since childhood from the influence of his idol Red-Haired Shanks.

Battles of King of Pirates

Luffy has battled a variety of world powers, from the most formidable pirates in the East Blue to the Marines, Seven Warlords of the Sea, Cipher Pol, World Nobles, and many others.

Previous Bounties of King of Pirates

After taking down the toughest pirate leaders of the East Blue, he earned his first reward of Beli 30,000,000, which grew to Beli 100,000,000 after taking down Crocodile in Alabasta. His bounty was raised to Beli 300,000,000 following his crew's attack of Enies Lobby and escape from it. Luffy was one of the eleven "Super Rookies," pirates who simultaneously crossed the Red Line with bounties of over Beli 100,000,000 just before the Summit War, thanks to his enormous bounty upon reaching the Sabaody Archipelago.

One Piece World

Maps are a crucial part of navigation in the universe of One Piece because they frequently show the geography of a single island, a group of islands, or even an entire ocean (in which case, they are sometimes referred as sea charts). In order to construct safe navigation routes, avoid natural dangers, or become lost, it is typically the responsibility of navigators to read and understand maps while on a voyage.
